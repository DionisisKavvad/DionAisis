[2026-04-18] DECISION: Build Gmail MCP inside a new `~/Projects/mcps/` monorepo rather than a standalone repo, and keep credentials + Claude registration at user level (`~/.mcps/<server>/` + `~/.claude.json`). | REASONING: Monorepo matches official Anthropic pattern (`modelcontextprotocol/servers`) and community best practice (Turborepo/npm workspaces).

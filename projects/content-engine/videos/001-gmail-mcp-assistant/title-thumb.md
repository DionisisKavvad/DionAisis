# Video 001 — Title & Thumbnail options

## Title options

### A (recommended)
**"Claude's Gmail connector can't archive emails. So I built my own."**
- 63 chars
- Concrete failure of known tool + personal response
- Curiosity (why can't it?) + authority (I fixed it)

### B
**"The official Claude connector is read-only. Here's the MCP that fixes it."**
- 73 chars (tight)
- Frames the gap directly, appeals to prosumer
- "MCP" in title signals dev-audience, filter

### C
**"I archived 380 emails in one prompt. Claude's connector can't do this."**
- 69 chars
- Outcome + factual contrast
- Works well on YouTube + Shorts clip titles

**Pick A.** Specificity of "can't archive" makes it click-worthy without clickbait. C is backup.
